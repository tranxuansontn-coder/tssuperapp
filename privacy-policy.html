Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

TS Global ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Ads Automation tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Facebook Access Tokens and Ad Account IDs that you provide to manage your advertising campaigns.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our tool, including campaign configurations and preferences.
  • Media Files: Video files you upload for use in advertising campaigns. These files are uploaded directly to Facebook's servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Create and manage Facebook advertising campaigns on your behalf
  • Upload media content to your Facebook Ad Account
  • Generate ad copy and creative content
  • Store your preferences locally in your browser

4. Data Storage

All configuration data (API keys, tokens, preferences) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not store any of your data on external servers. Your data remains on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Our tool interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Facebook Marketing API: To create and manage ad campaigns
  • Google Gemini API: To generate ad copy text

Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All API communications are conducted over HTTPS. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage. No data is retained on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
